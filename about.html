Passions of my personal life

Having a job that you love feels great, doing the thing you enjoy every day feels like it’s not working at all. But having even more passions that intersect with each other makes living even better. And that is the life I’m living, and life I’m enjoying. Outside of design, I’m actively working as a photographer. I do it as part of a photography duo called FOURTH, which is created by me and my beautiful girlfriend Lenka. Her love is another passion that helps me win every new day.

Analog photography, my lovely girlfriend, a job that I enjoy. Is there anything else that can make all that even better? Well, the secret is in healthy honest smiles and small things that make them. The small things that combine all the work and hobbies I love, the thing that can teach you how to find that happiness in little details.

I found those details in many things, and I keep exploring more. I started taking care of a few plants, learning about their needs and beauty. Bringing more nature that I love in my regular living space. I’m just starting a blog around my design experience, intending to help at least a few people start their creative journey. And at the end of the day, it’s just that simple. Think about the small thing that matter, and stay positive.
